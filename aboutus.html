About Everest Kitchen

Nepali Cuisine • Kamloops, British Columbia

Welcome to Everest Kitchen

Everest Kitchen is a family-owned Nepali restaurant in the heart of Kamloops. We bring the taste of the Himalayas with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Our dishes are prepared using traditional Nepali spices, fresh ingredients, and homemade flavours that remind you of warm hospitality back home. From momos to dal bhat, chowmein, and our special Himalayan curries — we serve everything with love.

Whether you are trying Nepali cuisine for the first time or craving a familiar taste, Everest Kitchen is excited to serve you.
